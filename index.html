About Our School

Our History

St Kizito SSS Nakibano was established in 1985 with a mission to provide quality education to students in the Nakibano region. Starting with just 50 students and 5 teachers, our school has grown to become one of the leading educational institutions in the area.

Over the years, we have expanded our facilities, enhanced our curriculum, and built a reputation for academic excellence and holistic education. Our graduates have gone on to excel in various fields, including medicine, engineering, education, business, and public service.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, holistic education that develops students intellectually, morally, socially, and physically, preparing them to be responsible citizens and leaders in their communities.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence and character formation that nurtures creative, critical thinkers and compassionate leaders who contribute positively to society.
